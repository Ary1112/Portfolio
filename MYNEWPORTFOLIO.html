About_Me

Hello, digital explorer. I'm Aaryan Kushwaha, a first-year Bachelor of Information Technology student at Model Institute of Technology in Baagbazar, Kathmandu.

Based in Chandragiri, Kathmandu, I'm passionate about exploring the vast landscape of cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and network defense systems. My journey in tech started with curiosity about how digital systems work and evolved into a focused pursuit of understanding security vulnerabilities and protection mechanisms.

Currently, I'm building a foundation in core IT principles while simultaneously diving into specialized areas like system penetration testing, network scanning, and secure coding practices. I believe that understanding both offensive and defensive security is essential to becoming a complete cybersecurity professional.

When I'm not coding or learning about new security exploits, you can find me participating in CTF challenges, exploring Linux systems, or reading about the latest tech breakthroughs.
